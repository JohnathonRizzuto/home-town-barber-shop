Our Story

A Hometown Chair. The Way It Should Be.

Home Town Barber Shop has been the spot Hinckley keeps coming back to since the year 2000. Locally owned, locally operated — we take the time to do every cut right and treat every man who walks through the door like a neighbor.

From classic men's cuts and beard work to old-school straight-razor shaves, we keep the craft simple and the standards high. Bring the kids. Bring your dad. Everyone walks out fresh.

"A real barber, a real chair, and the time to do it right."
